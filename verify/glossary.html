ROAS
Return on ad spend — revenue generated for every dollar of media spend.
Attribution
The method of assigning credit for a conversion across the touchpoints that influenced it.
CAC
Customer acquisition cost — total sales and marketing cost to win one new customer.
Full-funnel
A strategy that coordinates awareness, consideration, and conversion in one plan.
LTV
Lifetime value — the total revenue a customer is expected to generate over the relationship.
Answer engine
An AI system (LLM or search assistant) that returns a synthesized answer rather than a list of links.
Omnichannel
A coordinated presence across every channel a customer uses, with consistent messaging.
Lifecycle marketing
Nurturing customers across their journey with segmented, stage-appropriate messaging.
