FAQ

SEO and AI visibility, answered

What is AI visibility and how is it different from SEO?

SEO optimizes for ranked blue links inside Google. AI visibility, sometimes called AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) or GEO (Generative Engine Optimization), optimizes for inclusion inside AI-generated answers from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and Google AI Overviews. The technical foundations overlap heavily, but the success metric shifts from rank and clicks to citation and brand mention.

Do I still need traditional SEO if I invest in AI visibility?

Yes. AI engines lean heavily on the same crawl signals, schema, and authority that traditional SEO improves. Brands that already rank well in Google tend to be cited disproportionately in AI answers. The right model is layered, keep the SEO foundation strong and add an AI visibility layer on top.

What is AEO and how does it relate to GEO?

AEO stands for Answer Engine Optimization, structuring content so it can be lifted directly into an answer, whether that answer comes from a featured snippet, an AI Overview, or an AI chatbot. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is a closely related discipline focused specifically on large language model citations. Most engagements treat them as one program with shared deliverables.

How does schema actually help with ChatGPT and Google AI Overviews?

Structured data gives machines a precise, machine-readable definition of what each page is about, your organization, services, articles, FAQs, locations, and people. AI engines use these signals to disambiguate entities and decide which sources to cite. Schema is one of the highest-leverage investments because the work is one-time but compounds across every engine.

How do I know if ChatGPT or Perplexity is already citing my brand?

We combine server-log analysis (looking for GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, and Google-Extended user agents), prompt-based testing across the major engines, and brand-mention tracking tools to build a baseline citation map. The audit deliverable includes a current-state snapshot and the queries where you are, and are not, being mentioned.

Will Google AI Overviews cannibalize my organic traffic?

AI Overviews reduce clicks for purely informational queries, but they also create a new branded-mention surface that drives downstream consideration. The strategy is to defend revenue-aligned commercial keywords with deeper pages, and to structure informational content so your brand is the cited source inside the AI Overview itself.

How long until SEO and AI visibility work shows results?

Technical and schema fixes start appearing in AI engines within 2–6 weeks as the major bots re-crawl. Content and topical-authority work compounds over 60–120 days. Most engagements show measurable lift in branded mentions inside AI answers by month three, and meaningful organic ranking lift by months four to six.

Is this a fit for franchises and multi-location brands?

Yes, this is a core focus. The audit covers brand-level signals (Organization, authority, thought leadership) and location-level signals (LocalBusiness schema, Google Business Profile hygiene, location-page templates, reviews, and NAP consistency) so brand and locations reinforce each other in both Google and AI answers.
