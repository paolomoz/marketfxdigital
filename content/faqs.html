FAQ

Digital Marketing, SEO, AI Visibility, and Strategy FAQs

Answers, on the page, in plain English. For definitions of key terms, see our marketing glossary.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does marketFX Digital do?

marketFX Digital is a full-stack marketing agency that helps enterprise, franchise, and multi-location brands grow revenue through integrated strategy, paid media, SEO, AI visibility, analytics, content, CRM, and conversion optimization. Instead of stitching together five or six point vendors, clients get one accountable team operating against one strategy and one shared revenue scorecard.

Who does marketFX work with?

We work with enterprise brands, franchise networks, multi-location operators, professional services firms, DTC and ecommerce brands, and private-equity-backed portfolio companies across the United States and Canada. Engagements typically involve CMOs, VPs of Marketing, founders, or PE operating partners who need senior strategy and integrated execution without managing a dozen separate vendors.

What is a digital marketing audit?

A digital marketing audit is a structured review of how every channel is performing against revenue. We benchmark paid media accounts, SEO and AI visibility, web analytics and tracking, attribution, CRM and lifecycle programs, and creative, then quantify wasted spend, attribution gaps, and unrealized demand. The output is a prioritized 90-day roadmap, not a generic checklist.

What is included in a marketing audit?

A marketFX audit covers paid media account structure, creative and audiences, organic search and AI visibility, GA4 and server-side tracking, attribution modeling, CRM and lifecycle automations, landing pages and conversion funnels, and competitive positioning. You leave with a documented gap analysis, quantified opportunities, and a sequenced 30/60/90-day plan tied to pipeline and revenue targets.

How is marketFX different from a traditional agency?

Most agencies specialize in one channel and optimize for their own scorecard, which leaves clients refereeing between vendors. marketFX replaces the fragmented model with one integrated team that owns strategy, execution, and measurement across paid, organic, CRM, and analytics. Every initiative is tied back to pipeline and revenue, not impressions and clicks, one team, one P&L, full accountability.

Do you work with franchise and multi location brands?

Yes. A significant share of our work supports franchisors, multi-unit operators, and multi-location retail, healthcare, and services brands. We balance brand-compliant central strategy with local SEO, paid media, and reputation programs at the unit level, with reporting that lets corporate, regional, and franchisee stakeholders all agree on the same numbers.

Do you provide SEO and AI visibility audits?

Yes. We run technical SEO audits, content and topical authority audits, and AI visibility audits that score how often your brand is surfaced in AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude. The deliverable is a prioritized program covering technical fixes, content gaps, entity optimization, and the AEO and GEO work needed to be cited, not just ranked.

What is answer engine optimization?

Answer Engine Optimization, or AEO, is the practice of structuring content, schema, and entities so search engines and AI systems can extract direct answers and feature your brand in AI Overviews, featured snippets, and voice results. AEO emphasizes clear question-and-answer formatting, authoritative sourcing, and structured data, making your content the answer, not just a link in a list.

What is generative engine optimization?

Generative Engine Optimization, or GEO, is the discipline of getting your brand cited inside generative AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude. It combines technical SEO foundations, entity and topical authority, structured data, and high-quality cite-worthy content so AI systems pull and attribute your brand when answering buyer questions across the funnel.

What is included in a paid media audit?

A paid media audit reviews account structure, conversion tracking, audience strategy, bidding, creative, landing experience, and budget allocation across Google, Meta, LinkedIn, programmatic, and paid social. We quantify wasted spend, identify ROAS and CAC leakage, and deliver a restructured plan with sequenced experiments designed to lift return on ad spend within the first 60 to 90 days.

What is included in marketing analytics consulting?

Marketing analytics consulting covers GA4 implementation, server-side tracking, event taxonomy, conversion modeling, attribution, and executive dashboards in Looker Studio or your BI tool. We build the measurement layer that ties paid, organic, CRM, and sales pipeline together so leadership can reallocate budget with confidence, and so every channel is judged against revenue.

How long does a marketing audit take?

A focused marketFX audit typically takes two to four weeks, depending on the number of channels, locations, and platforms in scope. Week one is data access and discovery, weeks two and three are diagnostic review and quantification, and the final week is roadmap synthesis and an executive readout with prioritized initiatives, owners, and expected impact.

Do you help with implementation or only strategy?

Both. Some clients engage marketFX strictly for strategy, audit, and roadmap work and then execute internally with our SOPs. Most engage us for ongoing implementation, running campaigns, managing SEO and content, owning analytics, and coordinating creative, so the same team that designs the strategy is accountable for the results it produces.

How do you measure marketing performance?

We measure marketing performance against pipeline, qualified leads, ROAS, CAC, LTV, and contribution margin, not impressions and clicks. Every engagement is wired to a measurement framework with GA4, server-side tracking where appropriate, attribution modeling, and executive dashboards reviewed monthly so budget consistently flows to the highest-ROI channels.

How can a company start working with marketFX?

Most companies start with a 30-minute scoping call followed by a marketing audit. From there we propose either a focused engagement, for example a paid media audit or SEO program, or a full integrated retainer. You can request an audit or book a strategy call directly from the contact page; both kick off the same intake process.
