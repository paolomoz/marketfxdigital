Metro Vancouver context

prose

As a Vancouver marketing agency, we operate in one of the most sophisticated and demanding local markets in Canada. Metro Vancouver combines a mature technology corridor, a mature real estate and financial services sector, a globally recognised outdoor and lifestyle brand cluster, a busy DTC and retail landscape, and a hospitality economy anchored by YVR and the tourism corridor along the North Shore, Whistler, and the Sea to Sky. Consumer expectations here are shaped by exposure to some of the best brand marketing on the continent — which means the bar for the marketing agency Vancouver brands hire has moved considerably higher over the past decade.

The Vancouver agency landscape is crowded with boutique shops and freelancers, most of whom own a fragment of the marketing stack. That model produces a familiar pattern: a social manager here, an SEO consultant there, a media buyer somewhere else, and no single team accountable for the commercial outcome.

FAQs

Vancouver marketing agency FAQ

How much does a marketing agency in Vancouver cost?

Marketing agency pricing in Vancouver depends on scope, channels, and the level of strategic ownership you need. Most Metro Vancouver retainers we take on start in the mid-four figures CAD monthly for focused programs and scale from there. Because we consolidate strategy, media, SEO, content, and analytics under one team, total marketing spend typically stays flat or falls even as we take on additional channels.

What kinds of Vancouver brands do you work with?

Our Vancouver marketing agency practice includes DTC and retail brands like Stonz Wear, national franchise systems including our decade-long partnership with 7-Eleven Canada, luxury multi-location retailers such as Spence Diamonds, and institutional Metro Vancouver names including YVR, Cactus Club Cafe, and Vancity across various engagements over the past two decades.

Do you serve clients across British Columbia and the rest of Canada?

Yes. Our Vancouver office serves clients across Metro Vancouver — North Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, Surrey, Coquitlam — and across Canada. With a second office in Scottsdale, Arizona, we also run cross-border programs for Canadian brands expanding into the United States and American brands entering the Canadian market.

How does a Vancouver engagement start?

Every engagement begins with a working session and a marketing audit rather than a pitch deck. We review current performance, analytics, and internal team structure, then build a commercial roadmap that ties every channel to revenue targets. Only once the roadmap is agreed do we scope the retainer, cadence, and measurement framework.

What makes marketFX different from other Vancouver marketing agencies?

The Vancouver market is full of specialist boutiques — a social shop here, an SEO consultant there, a media buyer somewhere else. We run every marketing capability as one accountable team on one roadmap with one revenue-linked reporting layer. That eliminates the coordination tax of managing multiple vendors and ties every dollar back to commercial outcomes rather than channel-level activity metrics.
