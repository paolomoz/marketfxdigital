What Is CRM Automation

CRM automation defined

CRM and marketing automation is the system that moves a contact from first touch to repeat purchase without a person sending each message by hand. It combines your customer data, segmentation, and triggered email and SMS into flows that run on their own and respond to behavior. Done well, it pulls more revenue from people who already know you, which is almost always cheaper than buying more traffic.

The Full Lifecycle

Every stage of the customer lifecycle, automated and measured

We do not treat email as a channel off to the side. We map the full journey, from the first sign-up to the win-back two years later, and automate the moments that move revenue. Acquisition feeds it, the website converts it, and lifecycle keeps it compounding. One system, one set of numbers.
